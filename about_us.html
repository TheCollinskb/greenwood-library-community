About Greenwood Library

Founded in 1980, Greenwood Library has been the cornerstone of education and culture in the community. Our mission is to provide a welcoming space for learning, reading, and cultural exchange.

Our Services

  • Books and e-books for all age groups
  • Storytelling and reading programs for children
  • Community events and workshops
  • Free internet and computer access
